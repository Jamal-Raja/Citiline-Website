Where Personal Tax Becomes Personal Strategy

As your finances grow more complex, so do the decisions behind them. Whether you're a founder, landlord, professional, or globally mobile individual, managing your tax well isn’t just about avoiding mistakes — it’s about protecting what you’ve built and planning for what comes next.

At Citiline, we offer clear, forward-looking advice to help you structure your personal affairs with purpose. We don’t just fill forms — we help you understand the tax landscape around your income, assets, and ambitions. From self-assessment to succession, our goal is the same: to help you stay one step ahead, year after year.

What We Deliver

  • Self-Assessment Tax Returns: Efficient, accurate preparation and filing — with strategic oversight to ensure full compliance, reduce errors, and avoid unnecessary HMRC attention.
  • Income Tax Planning: Holistic planning for earnings across salaries, dividends, rental income, and overseas sources — structured to support flexibility and long-term efficiency.
  • Capital Gains Tax Advice: Guidance on the timing and structure of asset disposals — including property, shares, and crypto — to help minimise liability and access key reliefs.
  • Property Tax Structuring: Support for landlords and investors across ownership models, mortgage interest treatment, and tax-efficient planning for property acquisition or sale.
  • Inheritance Tax & Estate Planning: Advice on managing wealth transfer through gifting, trusts, and estate structures — designed to preserve assets and reduce the long-term IHT burden.
  • Non-Domicile & Cross-Border Tax: Strategic guidance on residency, remittance basis planning, and global income structuring — tailored to internationally mobile individuals and families.
  • Pension & Retirement Strategy: Planning around contributions, access, and long-term allowances — ensuring your pension works in line with your lifestyle goals and future needs.
