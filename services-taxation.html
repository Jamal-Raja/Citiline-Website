Understanding your needs

Navigating the business taxation landscape requires expertise that comes from years of experience and deep understanding of HMRC requirements. Whether you're looking to optimise your tax position, ensure compliance, or resolve disputes, our team brings the practical legal knowledge and ex-HMRC insight to maximise opportunities while minimising risks.

Our business taxation team understands the commercial pressures facing modern businesses. We work closely with our clients and their advisers to deliver practical, cost-effective solutions that align with your business objectives.

Major transactions typically carried out by our Taxation team include:

  • Corporate Tax Planning: Strategic advice to minimise corporation tax liabilities while ensuring full compliance with current legislation.
  • VAT Advisory: Comprehensive VAT planning, compliance, and dispute resolution services.
  • R&D Tax Credit Claims: Maximising relief for qualifying research and development activities.
  • Capital Allowances: Optimising tax relief on business assets and equipment purchases.
  • International Tax Compliance: Managing cross-border tax obligations and transfer pricing requirements.
  • HMRC Investigations: Expert representation during tax enquiries and dispute resolution.
