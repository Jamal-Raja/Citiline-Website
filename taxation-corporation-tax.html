Experts in Business Taxation

Slash your tax bill, not your ambitions — we build powerful, compliant strategies that fuel growth and protect every pound.

Corporation Tax is one of the most significant outgoings for UK companies — but it doesn’t have to be a passive cost. With the right strategy, it becomes a tool for long-term financial efficiency and control.

At Citiline Accountants, we specialise in Corporation Tax advisory for ambitious business owners. Our focus is not just on compliance, but on smart structuring, forward planning, and making full use of reliefs and exemptions that are built into UK tax law.

We work closely with directors and in-house finance teams to align every element of your company’s structure and operations with the latest Corporation Tax regulations — helping you legally minimise tax liability and reinvest more profit into your business.

Corporation Tax Areas We Advise On

  • Marginal Relief Planning: Optimising income timing, group structures, and deductions to reduce your effective Corporation Tax rate within tapered profit thresholds.
  • Capital Allowances: Strategic guidance on claiming full relief for qualifying plant and machinery, including full expensing and timing optimisation.
  • R&D Tax Relief: End-to-end support for identifying qualifying projects and submitting compliant, maximised claims to reduce Corporation Tax.
  • Loss Relief Strategy: Leveraging carry-back, carry-forward, and group relief provisions to convert trading losses into tax savings and recover overpaid Corporation Tax.
  • Associated Companies Rules: Structuring ownership and operations to mitigate the impact of associated company rules on Corporation Tax thresholds and tapering.
  • Group Relief and Intra-Group Planning: Tax-efficient planning of profit and loss transfers within corporate groups to reduce overall Corporation Tax exposure.
  • Substantial Shareholding Exemption (SSE): Ensuring your corporate structure qualifies for this exemption on trading subsidiary disposals to eliminate Corporation Tax on gains.

Corporation Tax Strategy – Not Just Filing

Most firms deal with Corporation Tax once a year. We work with you year-round. Our goal is to ensure you make well-timed, well-informed decisions that keep your tax position efficient and your business fully compliant.

That includes:

- Annual investment planning for maximum deductions
- Timing of income recognition to control tax exposure
- Managing trading losses to recover overpaid Corporation Tax
- Planning for acquisitions, disposals, or group restructuring with tax efficiency in mind
